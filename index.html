Dear Simple Apps community,

We are proud to announce that Simple Apps has been acquired by ShopStorm.

We started Simple Apps at Holstee to scratch our own e-commerce itches on Shopify, creating a suite of apps to improve the experiences of our own shop and of fellow Shopify shop-owners.

After growing the apps for two years, we are excited that they will continue to flourish under the new leadership of the ShopStorm team. Max, Justin and the ShopStorm team bring years of experience developing for ecommerce and will surely take these apps to new heights.

If you are already been using the Happy Ending App, I know they would love to hear your thoughts on how to take the app to the next level. If you have feature request or suggestions definitely get in touch with ShopStorm, you are in good hands :-)

Thanks again for all the support as we built Simple Apps. We hope the apps helped you simplify your growing businesses!

Best wishes,

Thomas Reggi (CTO, Holstee) and the entire Holstee Family
